STEVE WOZNIAK

This side is about Steve Wozniak

Wozniak was born in San Jose, California, in 1950. He received a B.S. in electrical engineering and computer science from UC Berkeley (1986). Wozniak (”Woz”) built his first computer when he was 13 years old, and was an electronics prodigy in high school.

Steve Jobs and Wozniak

In partnership with his friend Steve Jobs, Wozniak invented the Apple I computer. The pair founded Apple Computers in 1976 with Ronald Wayne, releasing some of the first personal computers on the market.

Wozniak

The co-inventor of the Apple Computer, still attends rock concerts frequently.
